The invited speaker for the opening keynote of the workshop will be [Patricia Feeney](https://www.crossref.org/people/patricia-feeney/) from Crossref.

Patricia Feeney oversees product support at Crossref and helps maintain and improve core services. During her 10 year career at Crossref she’s helped thousands of publishers understand how to record and distribute metadata for millions of scholarly items. She’s also worked in various scholarly publishing roles and as systems librarian and cataloger. Patricia has a Master of Library and Information Science from the University of Rhode Island and an MA in Publications Design from the University of Baltimore.
