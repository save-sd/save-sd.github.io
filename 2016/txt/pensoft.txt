[Pensoft Publishers](http://www.pensoft.net/) is a company specialized in academic book and journal publishing, software development, web design, dissemination and PR of science news. Since its foundation in 1994, Pensoft has published more than 800 books and e-books and in 2014 launched a novel Open Access book (re-)publishing platform, [Advanced Books](http://ab.pensoft.net/). Pensoft is well known among academics worldwide with its technologically advanced peer-reviewed Open Access journals, such as [ZooKeys](http://www.pensoft.net/journals/zookeys), [PhytoKeys](http://www.pensoft.net/journals/phytokeys), [MycoKeys](http://www.pensoft.net/journals/mycokeys), [Nature Conservation](http://www.pensoft.net/journals/natureconservation), [NeoBiota](http://www.pensoft.net/journals/neobiota), [Comparative Cytogenetics](http://www.pensoft.net/journals/comparativecytogenetics). 

The company is actively developing new tools, workflows and methods for text and data publishing, dissemination of scientific information and technologies for semantic enrichment of articleâ€™s content. As part of the EU FP7 project ViBRANT, the company has developed innovative and globally unique online data- and text-integrated publishing platform, with an entirely XML-based editorial workflow consisting of the collaborative manuscript authoring environment [Pensoft Writing Tool (PWT)](http://pwt.pensoft.net/) and the [Biodiversity Data Journal (BDJ)](http://biodiversitydatajournal.com/). Pensoft has also developed several tools for semantic publishing, such as the [Pensoft Markup Tool (PMT)](http://zookeys.pensoft.net/articles.php?id=2215), [Pensoft Taxon Profile (PTP)](http://ptp.pensoft.eu/), [Pensoft Wiki Concertor (PWC)](http://zookeys.pensoft.net/articles.php?id=2280), the literature and data discovery tool [Refindit.org](http://www.refindit.org), and others. Another major direction of the company is designing and development of websites and communication platforms, as well as publishing of various outreach materials (flyers, leaflets, brochures).
